My Favorite Musicians

1. Shakira

Shakira is a Colombian singer. She is well known for her songs Hips don't lie and La La La (Waka waka)
Shakira is married to Spanish soccer player Pique, and they have two sons: Milan and Sasha.
My favorite songs from Shakira are:

  1. Sale el Sol
  2. Gitana
  3. Antologia

You may not recognize these songs. That's because I like her old songs better than the new. My opinion is shared by most Colombians.
